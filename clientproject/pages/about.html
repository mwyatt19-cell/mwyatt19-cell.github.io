About Us

Welcome to Paws & Hearts Animal Shelter!

Our Mission

The mission of Paws & Hearts is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome dogs in need, giving them a second chance at a safe and loving life. We are dedicated to connecting every pup with a caring home while inspiring compassion within our community.

Our History

Founded in 2010, Paws & Hearts Animal Shelter has been a beacon of hope for abandoned and neglected dogs in our community. What started as a small group of volunteers has grown into a thriving organization dedicated to saving lives and finding loving homes for dogs in need.

What makes us special

What makes our shelter stand out is our commitment to being a no-kill, community-driven organization that gives every dog the time, care, and second chance they deserve. We also focus on rescuing at-risk and overlooked dogs, including those from neglect, abandonment, or urgent situations, and matching them with loving, lasting homes.
